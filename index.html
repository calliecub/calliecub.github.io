Callie Leone

I am an out-of-the-box thinker who likes to create solutions for everyday problems. I think
that the process is just as important as the end result in both design and problem-solving.

At NYU's Gallatin School of Individualized Study I created my own major by taking classes
in communications, business, media and philosophy. Through my interdisciplinary studies
I realized that design and content must go hand-in-hand to cultivate a healthy relationship
between a user and a company.

I am largely self-taught and came to the Startup Institute to solidify my skills in design so that I can create disruptive technology rather than be disrupted by it.
